OUR STORY

One trade, two brothers, a single obsession.

Ahmet and Dilaver Hasdemir came from Kayseri to Istanbul and began working as young painters on construction sites. The tools they needed simply didn't exist the way they wanted them — so they made their own.

When other tradesmen kept asking for those same tools, the Dekor brand was born in 1964. From a single product in an apartment basement, a patient industrial journey carried Dekor into the 21st century as part of the integrated Hassan Group.
